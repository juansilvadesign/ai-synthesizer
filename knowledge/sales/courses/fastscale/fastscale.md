## TL;DR

FastScale is a 1:1 applied sales mentoring program that extends Vexa's M.R.C. framework into a niche-specific execution layer for selling digital marketing services to psychologists. The program drills the R1+R2 two-call structure, builds a tiered product stack anchored on the Protocolo 7K, and introduces ICP-calibrated language rules (lead→paciente, marketing→captação) that reduce resistance from a high-empathy, analytically-cautious buyer profile. Its core insight is that the sale is decided across concordance cycles during qualification — not at the close.

---

## Core ideas

- The psychologist ICP (DISC: Analytical/Stable) buys on logic + security, not hype. Replace the "guruzão" register with calm authority and mission-framing.
- Every product in the stack exists to ensure no sale is lost: anchor high (P10K), sell core (P7K), cascade through VP → P3K → Plano 45D → GBP Scale.
- The "alarme ou notificação?" question is the single most powerful urgency trigger in the pitch — if the answer is "notificação," reinforce pain before presenting price.
- Concordance cycles pre-commit the lead: by the time price appears, the lead has already decided internally. The close only viabilizes a decision already made.
- The Unique Mechanism (GAP — Gerador de Agenda Previsível) differentiates the service from commoditized "tráfego pago" and sells hope with credibility.
- Camera management (eyes on lead, not screen) is a competitive differentiator — most closers look at their slides.
- The R1 call qualifies terrain (curiosidade vs. ação) and ends with a locked R2 commitment; R2 delivers the full pitch from where R1 left off.
- "Você não é seu cliente" — never price from your own financial frame; R$30k can be cheap for the right L3/L4 profile.
- NR-1 (2026 regulation) mandates companies to provide psychological support — a structural demand tailwind for the psychologist ICP.

## Frameworks / models

- **NEPQ** — qualification sequence: Necessidade → Evidência → Posicionamento → Compromisso. Seven ordered questions that lead the prospect to verbalize their own problem and commit before price is shown.
- **R1 + R2 Structure** — two-call model: R1 (SDR, 7–20 min) qualifies terrain, selects product, and books R2 with an attendance guarantee ("Você pode me garantir que vai comparecer? Eu trava minha agenda para te atender"); R2 opens where R1 ended and closes in session.
- **Protocolo 7K Product Ladder** — P10K anchor → P7K core (R$6.980) → VP first downsell (R$5.480) → P3K (R$2.480) → Plano 45D (R$1.980) → GBP Scale (R$1.480). Each rung targets a lead type (L1–L4). Lead L2 (cobra muito, paga pouco) is filtered out entirely.
- **GAP Mechanism** — Gerador de Agenda Previsível: four-stage funnel Consciência → Ativação → Aquisição → Conversão. Shown as a diagram for 3–5 seconds — goal is to create complexity perception, not explain.
- **Ciclos de Concordância** — progressive micro-commitment loops during qualification. Each "yes" deepens the lead's investment in the outcome. When the lead objects after the cycles, they contradict their own stated positions — "bater na incoerência."
- **Polarity Inversion Anchoring** — show a product 30–40% above the target price (P10K to sell P7K) so the target price feels proportionate. "Apresentei R$22.000, ela assustou. Ela foi olhar agências de R$1.500. De lá ela voltou."
- **Rotulação** — label the lead as someone who has already decided before asking. Affirm, don't ask. Avoid "né?" — it transforms affirmations into approval requests and destroys authority.
- **Efeito Halo** — communication quality (strategic pauses, no fillers, authoritative framing) creates a competence halo that transfers trust to the product. "Se esse cara fala bem, provavelmente entende muito do que faz."
- **ICP Language Map** — forbidden → required pairs: "lead" → "paciente"; "marketing digital" → "captação pelo online"; "fechamento" → "parceria"; "me paga" → "acesso ao benefício"; "dinheiro" → "ser recompensada pelo teu trabalho". Never connect revenue growth to money directly — always to the mission of reaching more people.

## Curriculum

1. **Strategic Planning & Predictable Calendar** — ICP profiling (DISC: Analytical/Stable), product stack design (L1–L4 lead types), NEPQ qualification framework, NR-1 opportunity mapping. _Exercise:_ Map lead types to product rungs and write ICP language substitutions for the full vocabulary.
2. **Product, Offer & Commercial Presentation** — offer construction (unique mechanism + aggressive guarantee + bonuses), do-it-yourself / done-with-you / done-for-you delivery models, ClickUp client pipeline (Onboarding → Ativo → Ascensão → Concluído). _Exercise:_ Write the P7K offer card: mechanism name, promise, deliverables, guarantee slide, and one bonus.
3. **Sales Script & Slide-by-Slide Pitch** — SDR script (R1 flow), 15-slide sequence from anchor to closing, GAP mechanism framing, slogan criteria (cabe em camiseta + boné). _Exercise:_ Deliver the pitch out loud slide by slide, practising fraseamento correto by lead profile (above vs. below CRP minimum).
4. **Offer Refinement & Closing Techniques** — product ladder cascade (cair o céu), NEPQ deep-dive, camera management (Alt+Tab, share tab not screen), pricing with juros as a negotiation lever. _Exercise:_ Rehearse the "alarme ou notificação?" trigger and the full R1→R2 transition script.
5. **Presentation Review & Benchmarking** — polarity inversion technique, concordance cycles, Protocolo ROI calculation, cross-niche benchmarking strategy. _Exercise:_ Calculate the ROI case for P7K and P3K for a specific lead profile; identify one cross-niche reference to import into the presentation.
6. **Live R1 Roleplay** — communication patterns (silence, rotulação, pausa estratégica, inversão de polo), live practice against a coached prospect. _Exercise:_ Record a full R1 with video-off and audio-off passes — mark fillers, "né?" instances, and approval-seeking moments.
7. **Presentation Adjustments & Strategic Vision** — real call debrief (three-decisor case, Protocolo 7K closed at R$5.480), ICP analogy toolkit, ecosystem model (assessoria → cases → mentoria → closers → assessoria). _Exercise:_ Map one past call where an analogy could have been used and write the sentence.

## Capabilities unlocked

- Run a complete R1 qualification call for the psychologist ICP, select the right product, and book R2 with a presence guarantee
- Deliver the 15-slide commercial presentation with camera on lead, zoom-directed attention, and ICP-calibrated language throughout
- Build a product stack with anchor, core offer, and downsell cascade mapped to lead types L1–L4
- Apply concordance cycles to pre-commit a lead before price is revealed, then close by bating incoerência
- Calculate and verbalize the ROI case for Protocolo 7K/3K for a specific lead (ticket × sessions × months × LTV)
- Present the GAP mechanism in 3–5 seconds to create complexity perception without triggering "I can do this alone"
- Reframe objections using ICP analogies: iPhone barato (price anchoring), Maldivas (expectation reset), Estrogonofe (bad execution ≠ bad category)
- Productize service delivery: checklist → playbook → scale without single-point dependency

## How to apply

Reach for this pack when:
- **Preparing a R1 or R2 call for a psychologist prospect** — the ICP Language Map prevents friction-inducing word choices; the NEPQ question bank provides the full qualification flow.
- **Designing or repricing a service offer** — the product ladder and ROI calculation tell you which rung to lead with and how to anchor above it.
- **Reviewing a recorded call** — use Efeito Halo and Ciclos de Concordância as diagnostic lenses for where authority or commitment broke down.
- **Training a new SDR or closer** — Capabilities unlocked is the training checklist; the Roleplay session notes (Module 6) are the drill protocol.
