## TL;DR

PsiAtiva's entire positioning rests on one axis: the problem is operational, not clinical. Excellent psychologists lose patients between the first contact and the confirmed session — not because they lack ability, but because they lack process. PsiAtiva is the named solution to that specific gap (GAP — Gerador de Agenda Previsível), positioned as an assessoria de processo, never a marketing agency. This distinction is the entire brand — every copy decision, every product name, every piece of vocabulary either reinforces or erodes it.

---

## When to use

- Writing landing page copy, proposals, pitch decks, Instagram content, or email cadences
- Deciding how to frame a new product, service, or campaign
- Onboarding a new team member or closer on PsiAtiva's commercial narrative
- Reviewing any outward-facing material for positioning consistency

## Prerequisites

- Brand vocabulary map loaded (`marca-identidade-visual.md`) — no forbidden terms in any copy
- ICP confirmed — the positioning emphasis shifts by profile: clinic responds to "previsibilidade e organização"; solo responds to "presença e alívio"

## Procedure — Constructing a Positioning-Consistent Message

1. **Open with the process gap** — locate the problem outside clinical competence.
2. **Name the mechanism** (GAP) as the solution — briefly, not explained in full.
3. **Adapt the promise to the ICP** (see promise table below).
4. **State the guarantee** before or alongside price — it eliminates the trust objection silently.
5. **Apply the NR-1 tailwind** when the context allows it — urgency without pressure.
6. **Run the positioning checklist** before the piece ships.

## The Core Problem Frame

> *"Psicólogos excelentes perdem pacientes por falta de estrutura entre o primeiro contato e a sessão confirmada. O papel da PsiAtiva é fechar esse buraco de forma ética, previsível e respeitosa com a identidade clínica do profissional."*

This framing does two things at once:
- Removes the ego threat ("não sei fazer marketing") by locating the problem outside clinical skill
- Positions the solution as organization, not salesmanship — which passes the ICP's ethics filter

**Never frame the problem as:** marketing ignorance, low visibility, or "falta de estratégia digital" — these trigger shame or defensiveness.

## The Unique Mechanism — GAP

**GAP — Gerador de Agenda Previsível**

Four-stage funnel owned by PsiAtiva. Shown briefly in the pitch to create differentiation perception — goal is complexity perception, not explanation (3–5 seconds).

```
Consciência → Ativação → Aquisição → Conversão
```

- **Consciência:** patient becomes aware of the psychologist (Google, Instagram, search)
- **Ativação:** professional profile is trustworthy enough to prompt contact
- **Aquisição:** first contact is received and answered (WhatsApp process)
- **Conversão:** contact becomes a confirmed appointment (confirmation + no-show prevention)

**Usage rule:** always written as "GAP — Gerador de Agenda Previsível" on first use. Never abbreviated alone. In copy, name it — don't diagram it. Over-explaining the mechanism domesticates it and removes the complexity signal.

| ICP | Core promise | Guarantee | Framing axis |
|---|---|---|---|
| Clínica com equipe | Adicionamos no mínimo R$10.000 de faturamento em até 6 meses | Ficamos até bater a meta, sem custo adicional | Logic + security: numbers, process, bounded risk |
| Psicóloga autônoma | Processo ativo em 45 dias — sem depender só de indicação | Ajustamos sem custo se os canais não gerarem contatos | Alívio + presença: something that works while she attends |

The guarantee must appear **before price** — it converts the trust objection from a blocker into a non-issue.

## Structural Tailwind — NR-1 2026

From January 2026, Brazilian companies are legally required to offer psychological support to employees (NR-1 occupational health update). This creates structural corporate demand for well-positioned psychologists.

**Usage:** opens well as urgency-without-pressure framing, especially for Perfil B.
> *"A partir de 2026, empresas vão ser obrigadas a oferecer suporte psicológico. Psicólogos bem posicionados online vão capturar essa demanda primeiro."*

**Do not force it** into contexts where the ICP is not thinking about corporate clients — the signal only works when the lead already sees the opportunity.

## Watch out for

- Framing growth as *"escalar"* or *"explodir a agenda"* — triggers ethics fear; use *"crescer com previsibilidade"* or *"agenda mais estável"*
- Letting the word "marketing" appear in any external copy — always replace with *"captação"* or *"processo de presença"*
- Letting the guarantee disappear from proposals and landing pages — it is the single most powerful trust signal for this ICP
- Treating both ICP types identically — clinic buys on revenue and organization; solo buys on relief and visibility; mixing messages dilutes both
- Over-explaining the GAP mechanism — brief complexity is the signal; full explanation removes the mystique

## Positioning Checklist

- [ ] Problem is framed as a process gap, not a competence gap
- [ ] PsiAtiva is positioned as assessoria, never agência
- [ ] GAP mechanism is named correctly (full form on first use)
- [ ] Guarantee appears before or alongside price
- [ ] ICP-calibrated language throughout (no "marketing", "leads", "vendas", "escalar")
- [ ] Promise matches the correct ICP (R$10k/6 meses for clinic; processo ativo/45 dias for solo)
- [ ] NR-1 tailwind is present where the context allows — not forced

## How to apply

- **In landing page / site copy:** open with the process problem frame; name GAP; show guarantee before price; adapt promise to the ICP the page targets.
- **In commercial presentations:** use the GAP mechanism briefly (3–5 seconds, don't explain it); build narrative from problem → mechanism → promise → guarantee → price.
- **In Instagram copy:** lead with hemorrhage framing (concrete loss); address the ethics concern every few posts to resolve the ICP's silent objection.
- **In proposals:** use the exact promise and guarantee language from `esteira-de-produtos.md` — never paraphrase the guarantee.
- **When onboarding a closer or SDR:** use the for/against table and the competitive differentiation table as the first mental model to install.
