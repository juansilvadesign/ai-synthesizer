## TL;DR

PsiAtiva's brand is built around a single positioning axis: clinical captation partner, not marketing agency. Every visual and verbal decision flows from this. The teal/sage palette, serif typography, and vocabulary map all serve one function — signal "high-standard private clinic," not "digital marketing startup." The compliance checklist at the end of this file is the mandatory decision gate before any artifact ships.

---

## When to use

- Creating or reviewing any PsiAtiva artifact: site, landing page, Instagram post/carousel/story, proposal deck, WhatsApp script, client report, or internal document.

---

## Prerequisites

- ICP confirmed (clinic or solo) to calibrate tone
- Vocabulary map loaded — forbidden words must never appear in external copy
- Font stack available: New York (Display), Lora (body), Source Serif Pro / DM Sans (UI)
- Color tokens resolved — see palette reference below

---

## Procedure

1. **Identify the artifact format** to apply the correct format rules: site/LP, Instagram post, Instagram carousel, presentation deck, or internal document.
2. **Set the background** using the mandatory color map:
   - Default page background: `#F0F7F4` (off-white)
   - Alternate sections: `#E4E6E3` (sage BG)
   - Dark sections / hero: `#1C2B2B`
   - **Never** use pure white `#FFFFFF` — always `#FAFFFF` (warm white) or `#F0F7F4`
3. **Apply CTA colors:** primary = `#1A4B51` bg + `#FAFFFF` text; hover = `#266D74`; outline = `#1A4B51` border + transparent bg.
4. **Select typography by level:**
   - Headlines / Display: New York
   - Body copy: Lora
   - Labels, buttons, UI micro-copy: Source Serif Pro or DM Sans
   - **Forbidden fonts:** Inter · Roboto · Arial · Open Sans · Space Grotesk · Helvetica · Montserrat · Poppins
5. **Scan copy for forbidden vocabulary** — replace using the vocabulary table below.
6. **Calibrate tone by context:**
   - Landing page / site: firme, empático, técnico → *"Agenda previsível começa com processo — não com sorte."*
   - Instagram posts: educativo, próximo, direto → *"Você não perdeu aquele paciente por falta de qualidade. Perdeu por falta de processo."*
   - WhatsApp / e-mail: próximo, sem pressão → *"Queria entender melhor o momento da sua clínica antes de qualquer coisa."*
   - Presentation: confiante, estruturado, com autoridade
7. **Replace improvement language with hemorrhage language** — frame as concrete loss, not vague benefit (see table below).
8. **Add WhatsApp CTA** on all digital formats with pre-filled message: *"Olá! Vim pelo site da PsiAtiva e gostaria de agendar o diagnóstico gratuito da minha clínica."*
9. **Run compliance checklist** before delivery.

**If** artifact is mobile-facing (site, LP, Instagram): mobile-first layout + floating WhatsApp CTA in footer.
**If** artifact uses dark background (`#1C2B2B` or `#1A4B51`): switch logo to negative (white) variant.
**If** urgency signal needed: use `#F7C800` (yellow) — never red.
**If** animations present: fade-in + 8–12px slide, `ease-in-out`, 200–300ms; no pop-ups, no countdown timers.

---

## Watch out for

- Forbidden fonts (Inter, Roboto, Arial, etc.) — they signal "generic startup" or "marketing course" to the ICP
- Vocabulary leakage: `marketing` · `leads` · `vendas` · `funil` · `escalar` · `contrato` · `comercial` — none in external copy
- Improvement language (*"melhore sua captação"*) instead of hemorrhage language (*"cada semana com buraco na agenda é dinheiro que não volta"*)
- Photos of business-success poses, money visuals, growth charts, rockets — these destroy trust with the DISC Analytical+Stable ICP
- Using pure `#FFFFFF` — always warm white `#FAFFFF` or off-white `#F0F7F4`
- Filled/solid icons — always line icons, stroke 1.5–2px
- Non-proportional logo scaling or placing logo on low-contrast backgrounds
- Logo lock-up is not yet finalized (v1.0 note) — use Ideogram-generated versions until final lock-up is approved

**Hemorrhage framing (always preferred over improvement framing):**

| ❌ Improvement (avoid) | ✅ Hemorrhage (use) |
|---|---|
| *"Melhore sua captação"* | *"Cada semana com buraco na agenda é dinheiro que não volta"* |

| *"Cresça seu consultório"* | *"Depender só de indicação é apostar que o mês vai ser bom"* |

## ICP Visual Reference

**Who she is:** Psychologist, clinic owner, 28–45 years, 2–15 professionals, DISC Analytical+Stable. Bad prior experience with "marketing agencies." WhatsApp is her primary channel.

**Her emotional state at brand contact:** Tired of unpredictable schedule. Distrustful of promises. Afraid of appearing "mercenary" by using commercial approaches.

**She does not want to see/read:** *"Escale seu negócio" · "Leads qualificados" · "Marketing digital" · linguagem de guru de Instagram*

**She wants to see/read:** *"Agenda previsível" · "Sem perder a essência clínica" · "Processo, não improviso" · "Mais pacientes certos, não mais correria"*

**Success criterion for any artifact:** *"Isso foi escrito/feito por quem entende de clínica — não por quem vende curso."*

## Compliance Checklist

**Copy:**
- [ ] No forbidden words (`marketing` · `leads` · `vendas` · `contrato` · `escalar` · `funil` · `comercial` · `conversão`)
- [ ] Slogan present where applicable: *"Ativa sua clínica. Preserva sua essência."*
- [ ] GAP named correctly as *"Gerador de Agenda Previsível"* when cited
- [ ] Hemorrhage language, not improvement language
- [ ] Tone matches the artifact context (site vs. Instagram vs. WhatsApp vs. presentation)

**Visual:**
- [ ] Teal/sage palette — no colors outside the approved set without justification
- [ ] No forbidden fonts (Inter · Roboto · Arial · Open Sans · Space Grotesk · Montserrat · Poppins)
- [ ] Line icons only (stroke 1.5–2px, not filled)
- [ ] No imagery that remits to marketing agency, infoproduct, or growth-hacking culture

**Digital:**
- [ ] At least one clear CTA
- [ ] WhatsApp CTAs carry the pre-filled message
- [ ] Layout mobile-readable
- [ ] Animations (if any) ≤ 300ms, non-aggressive, no pop-ups or fake countdown timers

**Identity:**
- [ ] Artifact is immediately recognizable as PsiAtiva
- [ ] Artifact could not be mistaken for a marketing agency, online course, or generic consultancy

---

## How to apply

- **Before writing copy:** load the vocabulary map and hemorrhage framing table; scan every draft against both before output.
- **Before designing:** confirm font stack, background token, and ICP emotional state — every choice must pass the "high-standard private clinic" test, not the "startup" test.
- **Before delivery:** run the compliance checklist in full.
- **In AI-generated copy or design briefs:** use verbatim Portuguese phrases from the vocabulary map; never output improvement-language framing or forbidden brand vocabulary.
