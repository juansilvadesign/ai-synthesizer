## TL;DR

PsiAtiva's commercial process is a consultative, NEPQ-based system built for a psychologist ICP (DISC: Analytical/Stable). It runs across two stages — R1 qualification and R2 presentation — using a structured question sequence that leads the prospect to verbalize her own problem, calculate the cost of inaction, and commit before price is shown. Twelve high-performance principles govern behavior from the first second of eye contact through the in-call close. The sale always closes in the meeting; "vou pensar" is treated as a technical failure to diagnose.

---

## When to use

- Running or preparing an R1 (qualification) or R2 (full presentation) call with a psychologist prospect
- Reviewing a recorded call for performance gaps
- Training a new closer or SDR on PsiAtiva's process

---

## Prerequisites

- ICP confirmed: psychologist with an established patient base, charging above R$150/session, with at least R$500/month capacity for ads
- Lead terrain confirmed (curiosidade vs. ação) — do not run the full R2 pitch for a "curiosidade" lead without first moving her toward ação
- Calculator ready (browser or phone) for the live revenue-gap demonstration
- Presentation deck loaded and tested — share the browser tab, not the screen; camera aimed at the lead, not the slides
- One genuine point-in-common identified from the lead's profile before the call begins

## Procedure

### Phase 1 — Connection & Rapport (2–5 min)

1. Enter with high energy, smile, confirm technical setup: *"Você está me ouvindo e me vendo bem, 100%?"*
2. Request recording authorization quickly and move on.
3. Deliver one genuine point-in-common (city, specialty, shared experience) — **do not skip**.
4. Personal branding in under 60 seconds: your background, niche, and why you work with psychologists.
5. Set **Intent Agreement #1**: *"Meu objetivo aqui é entender a sua realidade. Posso te fazer algumas perguntas?"*

### Phase 2 — NEPQ Qualification (10–20 min)

Run the question sequence in this exact order. Always end on the positive (Solução/Comprometimento).

**Conexão — map the terrain:**
- *"Me fale de maneira resumida — quem é você, sua trajetória e qual sua expectativa com essa conversa?"*
  — Calibrate: prolixo = harder to close; objective = already knows what she wants.
- *"Como funciona seu processo de captação de pacientes atualmente?"*
- *"Há quanto tempo você vem captando dessa maneira?"*

**Problema — surface the pain:**
- *"O que não está funcionando do jeito que deveria?"*
- *"O que te impede de chegar no próximo nível?"*
- *"Se continuar assim, o que tende a acontecer?"*

**Impacto — amplify the cost of inaction:**
- *"Em relação ao ticket médio por consulta, quanto você acha que isso está te custando por mês?"*
  — **Calculator technique:** open calculator live; enter current patients × session fee × months; calculate the delta with 3–4 new patients at the new VPS. Show the number on screen. Let the prospect see her own lost revenue.
- *"Se as coisas continuarem assim, você atinge sua meta nos próximos 3 a 6 meses?"*
- *"Isso está te deixando mais próxima ou mais distante do que você quer?"*

**Solução — open the future:**
- *"Se a gente estalar o dedo agora e você tivesse atingido sua meta — o que muda na sua vida? O que você consegue fazer que hoje está incapacitada?"*
- *"Isso mudaria apenas o financeiro ou também sua qualidade de vida?"*

**Comprometimento — lock the terrain:**
- *"Resolver isso é sua prioridade hoje?"*
  — **Rotulação transition:** *"Bom, quando chegamos nesse momento, entendemos que você já tomou a decisão de que precisa investir em captação."*
- *"O que eu preciso te mostrar aqui hoje para que você entenda que minha empresa é a decisão certa para o seu negócio?"*
- *"Pensando em um cenário onde a gente avança — você tem mais alguém que te ajuda a tomar essa decisão?"*
  - **If** co-decision-maker absent → reschedule R2 with all decision-makers present. Do not proceed.

6. Set **Intent Agreement #2**: *"Com base em tudo que você me contou, posso te mostrar como funcionaria para o seu caso?"*

### Phase 3 — Pitch (15–30 min)

7. Present in this order: company credibility (Ethos) → methodology → product. Never lead with product.
8. Use voice as the primary tool: vary pace, volume, and tone intentionally. A good idea delivered flatly loses to a mediocre idea delivered with conviction.
9. Direct attention with voice and zoom — never use the cursor pointer.
10. Mid-pitch temperature check: **"Alarme ou notificação?"** — if "notificação," reinforce the pain from Phase 2 before moving to price.
11. Set **Intent Agreement #3** before price: *"Se eu te mostrar uma solução que resolve exatamente isso que você descreveu, você estaria aberta a explorar?"*

### Phase 4 — Anchoring (5 min)

12. Present the anchor product (P10K) first to invert polarity — P7K must feel proportionate by contrast: *"Apresentei R$22.000, ela assustou. Depois, R$6.980 pareceu acessível."*
13. Show the full-value tally before any discount. Never use internal terms (VT, VP, ancoragem) out loud.

### Phase 5 — Closing & Negotiation

14. State price → *"Isso aqui funciona para você?"* → stop screen share → 7–12 seconds of silence. Let the discomfort work.
    - **If** yes → proceed to payment immediately. Do not end the call until payment is confirmed.
    - **If** objection → go to step 15.
15. Defend table value first. Isolate until only the financial remains: *"É só a condição financeira que te impede de fechar hoje?"*
16. Present differentiated condition with a clear expiry. Get explicit agreement before revealing it: *"Ficou claro que saindo dessa call essa condição não vale mais?"*
17. **If** still no → cascade: add bonus → VP (R$5.480) → Protocolo 3K (R$2.480) → Plano 45D → GBP Scale.
18. Collect payment on the call. Never trust *"faço depois"* or *"amanhã cedo"*. The conversion of unpaid commitments is under 10%.

## Watch out for

- Presenting the product before completing the question sequence — selling before the prospect felt the pain makes every price objection harder.
- Skipping the authority check — closing a non-decision-maker creates the *"vou ver com meu sócio"* blocker at step 14.
- Using forbidden language: "lead", "marketing digital", "fechamento", "me paga". Required substitutions: "paciente", "captação", "parceria", "acesso ao benefício".
- Saying "né?" — it converts affirmations into approval requests and destroys authority in real time.
- Starting negotiation at the minimum price — always open at anchor → table value → VP. Never lead with the floor.
- Ending the call without a clean decision. *"Vou pensar"* is not a "no" to accept — diagnose which of the 6 certainties (problem, priority, solution, trust, investment, timing) is missing.

## Frameworks / models

- **NEPQ** (Neuro-Emotional Persuasion Questioning — Jeremy Miner) — consultative sequence where the prospect sells herself via structured questions. Phases: Conexão → Situação → Problema → Implicação → Consequência Emocional → Visão de Solução → Comprometimento. Product is presented only after all certainties are built.
- **GPCT BA CI** — qualification backbone: Goals → Plans → Challenges → Timeline → Budget → Authority → Consequences → Implications. Always end on Implications (positive pole) to close the qualification on a high emotional note.
- **3 Mental States** — Problem State (recognizes pain) → Solution State (visualizes resolution) → Commitment State (decides to act). The question sequence is designed to move the prospect through all three before price appears.
- **12 High-Performance Principles** — behavioral standards covering pre-call appearance (Principle 4), voice cadence (Principle 5), adaptability as a "camaleão" (Principle 7), closing posture as "modo buda" (Principle 8), and strategic silence (Principle 9).
- **Calculator Technique** — live revenue-gap demonstration: current patients × VPS × months, then delta with 3–4 new patients at target VPS. Makes the cost of inaction a concrete number on the screen.
- **Rotulação Transition** — before asking for commitment, label the prospect as someone who has already decided: *"Entendemos que você já tomou a decisão de que precisa investir em captação."* Activates cognitive coherence — the prospect confirms rather than deliberates.

## How to apply

- **Before a call:** confirm prerequisites, identify one point-in-common, pre-load the calculator, and review the question bank for this ICP.
- **During R1:** follow Phase 2 in exact order; do not improvise sequence or skip the authority check.
- **During R2:** run Phases 3–5 as a strict sequence; closing in-meeting is non-negotiable.
- **After a call:** use the 3 Mental States and NEPQ phases as a diagnostic lens — identify exactly which state the prospect stalled in and which question was missing.

The AI move: use this playbook to generate R1 scripts, WhatsApp cadences, or slide copy for PsiAtiva — pull exact Portuguese phrases from the question banks to preserve ICP-calibrated language and avoid AI-generic copy markers.
