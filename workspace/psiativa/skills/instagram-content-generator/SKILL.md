# Instagram Content Generator — PsiAtiva

Generates on-brand Instagram content for PsiAtiva's own account. The account speaks to psychologists — clinic owners and solo practitioners — who are skeptical of digital marketing and afraid of appearing commercial. Every post either reinforces the "assessoria de processo, not agência de marketing" positioning or erodes it. This skill generates content that could never be confused with a marketing course or a growth-hacking account.

**Success criterion for every piece generated:** *"Isso foi escrito por quem entende de clínica — não por quem vende curso."*

## Input

Required:
- **Pillar** (1–7 below, or "suggest" to pick the next one in rotation)
- **ICP** — Clínica / Solo / Mixed
- **Format** — post single / carousel / reels hook / story

Optional:
- Raw pain signal or specific case to anchor the content (*"psicóloga perdeu 3 pacientes em uma semana no WhatsApp"*)
- External trigger (NR-1 2026, seasonal — início de ano, retorno de férias, etc.)
- Specific GAP stage to educate (Consciência / Ativação / Aquisição / Conversão)

If no pillar is provided: check the pillar rotation rule in Step 1 and suggest the next one due.

## The 7 Content Pillars

Every PsiAtiva Instagram post belongs to one of seven pillars. Each serves a specific function in the positioning. Rotate through them — never run the same pillar twice in a row.

| # | Pillar | Function | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Diagnóstico de perda** | Surface the invisible loss — makes the ICP recognize herself | 2× per week (highest impact) |
| 2 | **Reframe do problema** | Process gap, not competence gap — removes shame and self-blame | 1× per week |
| 3 | **Objeção ética** | Captação ≠ mercantilização — pre-empts the ICP's deepest silent objection | 1× per 2 weeks |
| 4 | **Posicionamento de categoria** | Assessoria ≠ agência — eliminates wrong-category associations before they form | 1× per 2 weeks |
| 5 | **Prova e resultado** | Case result in compliant language — shows what change looks like concretely | 1× per week |
| 6 | **Educação de processo** | One GAP stage, one process insight — builds authority without explaining everything | 1× per week |
| 7 | **Chamada para conversa** | Low-commitment CTA — turns lurkers into conversations | 1× per week |

**Rotation rule:** never run Pillar 1 twice without at least one Pillar 2, 3, or 4 between them. Never run Pillar 7 more than once per week. Pillars 3 and 4 are high-stakes positioning pieces — run one at a time, never back-to-back.

## Step 1 — Select and Brief the Pillar

Map the user's input to a pillar. If no pillar is specified, pick the next one in the rotation based on what was last generated.

For each pillar, confirm which pain and desire vocabulary to load. Do not write a single sentence before the ICP vocabulary is active.

---

## Step 2 — ICP Vocabulary Load

Switch vocabulary context before drafting.

### Clínica (Perfil A)
Pain language: *"agenda oscilante" · "semana morta" · "recepção não dá conta" · "minha equipe não tem processo" · "faturamento imprevisível" · "apago incêndio o dia todo"*
Desire language: *"agenda previsível" · "faturamento estável" · "crescer sem caos" · "clínica funcionando sem depender de mim"*
**Never use:** individual exhaustion hooks, invisibility hooks, WhatsApp-while-in-session → those belong to Perfil B

### Solo (Perfil B)
Pain language: *"quando saio da sala a mensagem já foi" · "sou eu que respondo tudo" · "ninguém me acha no Google" · "mês bom e mês ruim sem previsão" · "sou a terapeuta e a secretária ao mesmo tempo"*
Desire language: *"responder enquanto estou em sessão" · "aparecer no Google" · "agenda sem depender só de indicação" · "cobrar o que mereço sem perder paciente"*
**Never use:** team language, receptionist references, faturamento de equipe → those belong to Perfil A

### Mixed
Use only ICP-neutral language that works for both: *"agenda imprevisível" · "paciente que chega e some" · "depender só de indicação" · "processo entre o contato e a consulta confirmada" · "primeira semana do mês"*
Avoid anything that requires a team OR assumes solo operation.

## Step 3 — Format Structure

Apply the correct format template for the selected pillar.

---

### Format A — Single Post

Structure:
1. **Hook** (first line, counterintuitive or hemorrhage-framed) — max 10 words
2. **Body** — 3–4 short sentences. One idea per sentence. No padding phrases.
3. **CTA** — one line. Low-commitment (see CTA mechanics below).

**Hook rule:** the ICP is the subject of the first sentence. PsiAtiva is never mentioned in the opening.

**CTA mechanics:** the action must feel like a natural next step, not a close.
- ❌ *"Garanta sua vaga agora!"* → ✅ *"Fala com a gente e entenda como funciona pra sua clínica."*
- ❌ *"Clique e transforme!"* → ✅ *"Quando fizer sentido, a gente está aqui."*
- ❌ *"Não perca essa oportunidade!"* → ✅ *"Primeiro passo: uma conversa. Só isso."*

---

### Format B — Carousel

Six slides per carousel. Each slide has one job. Never stack two ideas in a single slide.

#### Carousel Pillar 1 — Diagnóstico de perda

| Slide | Content |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Bold hemorrhage hook** — the specific loss, named directly. Max 8 words. |
| 2 | **The mechanism** — how the loss happens, step by step. *"Ela chega. Você não responde a tempo. Ela vai para quem respondeu primeiro."* |
| 3 | **The invisible cost** — concrete: per patient, per week, per month. Never vague. |
| 4 | **What makes it worse** — the compounding factor (dependência de indicação, sazonalidade, concorrente com processo) |
| 5 | **The reframe** — process gap, not quality gap. One sentence. |
| 6 | **CTA slide** — low-commitment. Dark background `#1C2B2B`, white text, WhatsApp CTA. |

#### Carousel Pillar 2 — Reframe do problema

| Slide | Content |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Counterintuitive claim** — not what she expects to hear |
| 2 | **The shame trap** — the belief she has about why the agenda is unstable (usually blames herself, her specialty, her price) |
| 3 | **Where the problem actually lives** — one specific operational gap in the patient journey |
| 4 | **What the gap costs** — hemorrhage framing (loss, not missed opportunity) |
| 5 | **The repositioned identity** — she is excellent at the clinical work; the missing piece is structural |
| 6 | **CTA slide** |

#### Carousel Pillar 3 — Objeção ética

| Slide | Content |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Name the fear directly** — *"Parecer comercial é o maior medo de quem ama o que faz."* |
| 2 | **What "comercial" actually means** — pushy, impersonal, misaligned with the therapeutic relationship |
| 3 | **What processo actually is** — organization that ensures the patient who needs you reaches you; not persuasion |
| 4 | **The flip** — not having process is also a choice. Someone pays for it. |
| 5 | **The ethics frame** — organized response is care, not sales pressure |
| 6 | **CTA slide** — *"Se você não quer parecer comercial mas quer uma agenda mais estável, a gente precisa conversar."* |

#### Carousel Pillar 4 — Posicionamento de categoria

| Slide | Content |
|---|---|
| 1 | **The category contrast hook** — *"Agência traz contato. Assessoria faz o contato virar consulta confirmada."* |
| 2 | **Why generic agencies fail this ICP** — no niche knowledge, no WhatsApp process, no confirmation cadence |
| 3 | **What process-first actually means** — the 4 stages of the patient journey (GAP — named, not diagrammed) |
| 4 | **What PsiAtiva is not** — the is-not list in compliant language |
| 5 | **What PsiAtiva is** — one sentence, assessoria de processo |
| 6 | **CTA slide** |

#### Carousel Pillar 5 — Prova e resultado

| Slide | Content |
|---|---|
| 1 | **The before state** — one sentence. Concrete, ICP-recognizable, no jargon |
| 2 | **The specific gap** — what was broken in the process |
| 3 | **What was implemented** — brief; no methodology names; no forbidden vocabulary |
| 4 | **The after state** — specific, measurable, compliant (agenda / faturamento / confirmação — never "conversão" or "leads") |
| 5 | **The reframe** — what the result actually proves (process beats luck, small intervention = large impact) |
| 6 | **CTA slide** |

#### Carousel Pillar 6 — Educação de processo

| Slide | Content |
|---|---|
| 1 | **The counterintuitive insight** — what most people believe vs. what is actually true |
| 2 | **The mechanism** — one stage of the patient journey, how it works |
| 3 | **Where psychologists lose patients at this stage** — specific, not generic |
| 4 | **What a process at this stage looks like** — concrete; no methodology pitch |
| 5 | **The implication** — what happens when this stage is left unstructured |
| 6 | **CTA slide** — *"Quer entender como isso se aplica à sua clínica?"* |

---

### Format C — Reels Hook Script

Structure: the first 3 seconds that stop the scroll + optional sub-hook.

```
[HOOK — 1 sentence, spoken to camera or on-screen text]
[PAUSE or cut]
[SUBHOOK — one sentence that creates the question that makes her stay]
```

**Hook rules:**
- Never open with *"Olá"*, *"Hoje vou falar sobre"*, or *"Você sabia que"* — these are scroll-past openers
- Start with the loss, the mistake, or the counterintuitive claim
- Use *você* directly — personal address stops the scroll better than declarative statements
- The hook must create a question that makes her want the next 10 seconds

---

### Format D — Story

Stories are lighter and more personal than feed posts.

Rules:
- One idea only — if it needs two ideas, it needs two stories
- Question format works: poll, question sticker, or rhetorical
- Does not need a CTA — the feed post carries that load
- Never explain the GAP mechanism in a story — too short, creates confusion instead of complexity signal
- Can tease a carousel: *"Carrossel de quinta explica como você provavelmente está perdendo paciente sem saber."*

## Step 5 — Compliance Scan

Run every draft through these four checks before output. If one fails, rewrite before delivering.

| Check | Passes | Fails — fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Vocabulary** | Zero instances of: marketing · leads · vendas · funil · escalar · contrato · comercial · conversão · cliente (do psicólogo) · agência | Replace using vocabulary map in `marca-identidade-visual.md` |
| **Framing** | Every benefit is stated as a loss stopped, not a gain acquired | Replace improvement language: *"melhorar captação"* → *"parar de perder paciente no WhatsApp"*; *"atrair mais"* → *"deixar de perder os que já chegam"* |
| **Opening subject** | First sentence: ICP is the subject, not PsiAtiva | Rewrite to start with *"você"*, a pain signal, or a counterintuitive claim |
| **CTA tone** | Low-commitment; does not pressure or create false urgency | Replace: *"Garanta sua vaga"* → *"Fala com a gente"*; *"Não perca"* → *"Quando fizer sentido pra você"* |

## Watch out for

- **Opening with PsiAtiva's name** — the ICP reads the first sentence to decide if she stays. If the first word is "A PsiAtiva", she's already gone. Always open with her problem, her situation, or a claim that makes her stop.
- **Improvement framing creeping in** — *"melhore sua captação"*, *"atraia mais pacientes"*, *"cresça seu consultório"* are all banned. Rewrite as hemorrhage: what is she losing today without this?
- **Exclamation marks** — calm authority doesn't need them. Zero in body copy. One per page as absolute maximum.
- **Stacking two pillars in one post** — a post that diagnoses the pain AND explains the solution AND handles the ethics objection AND pitches PsiAtiva is a mess. One post, one job.
- **Using "marketing" in any context, even critically** — *"o marketing digital que não funciona"* still places "marketing" adjacent to PsiAtiva. Replace: *"a presença online que não converte"*, *"o investimento em anúncio sem processo"*.
- **GAP mechanism in a single post caption** — GAP is a complexity signal, appropriate for a carousel dedicated to positioning (Pillar 4 or 6). In a single post caption it reads as jargon without context.
- **Writing a post that could work for any healthcare business** — PsiAtiva copy only makes sense for psychologists. Test: remove "psicóloga" and "clínica de psicologia" from the post. If it still works, it's not specific enough. Rewrite.
- **Reels hooks starting with "Olá", "Hoje vou falar sobre", or "Você sabia que?"** — all scroll-past openers. Prefer direct loss statements or counterintuitive claims.
- **Solo copy with team language** — *"sua recepção"*, *"sua equipe"*, *"implementar com o time"* — solo practitioners have none of these. Disqualifies the post for Perfil B immediately.
- **Clinic copy with individual exhaustion hooks** — *"sou eu que faço tudo"*, *"sou terapeuta e secretária ao mesmo tempo"* — this is Perfil B's pain inventory. Clinic posts stay at the operational and team level.
- **Ending with a rhetorical question that answers itself** — *"Quer uma agenda mais previsível? Então..."* reads as manipulative to a DISC Analytical reader. Let the CTA be an invitation, not a trap.
