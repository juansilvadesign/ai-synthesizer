# Strategic Pro-Bono Planner — PsiAtiva

A deal-design skill for two scenarios that share the same logic: work without monetary payment is only strategic when the "currency" is a defined commercial asset. A testimonial, a case study, three network introductions, or a post to 8,000 followers — these are all currency. Gratitude is not.

**Portfolio mode:** trade work for proof assets (case study, testimonial, authorization) — applicable when PsiAtiva needs cases in a new niche or with a new product.  
**Media-for-equity mode:** trade work for audience access or advertising placement — applicable when a partner has reach over PsiAtiva's ICP that would cost more to buy than to earn.

## Input

Required:
- **Partner profile** — who they are, what their reach or network looks like, what their business problem is
- **Mode** — Portfolio (proof assets) or Media-for-Equity (audience/advertising)
- **Deliverable hypothesis** — what you already see as the specific problem you'd solve

If the deliverable hypothesis is unclear: run the 2-minute diagnostic on their presence first.

---

## Step 1 — Partner Qualification

Before designing the deal, disqualify first. A wrong partner produces the worst outcome: work completed, no proof, no audience, no commercial future.

### Who qualifies for Portfolio Mode

| Partner profile | Why they qualify |
|---|---|
| Established clinic with 10+ Google reviews and a visible presence problem | Generates real before/after, real testimonial, real case — with authority |
| Solo practitioner with 3+ years of practice and an active online presence | Produces a believable case in the target ICP segment |
| Psychology association, supervision group, or training institute | Produces social proof that reaches hundreds of professionals at once |
| Respected professional with a referral network in the niche | Introductions are worth more than impressions |
| NGO or non-profit with legitimate cause and real operational presence | Generates case with proof of reasoning without commercial pressure |

### Who qualifies for Media-for-Equity Mode

| Partner profile | Why they qualify |
|---|---|
| Instagram account with 5,000+ followers that is the PsiAtiva ICP (psychologists) | One post = direct exposure to prospects |
| WhatsApp group coordinator with 500+ members in target niche | A single recommendation post = trusted word-of-mouth in a closed environment |
| Psychology podcast or YouTube channel with consistent audience | Episode = long-form authority exposure that compounds |
| CRP regional body or equivalent professional organization | Endorsement = institutional credibility that cannot be bought |
| Health/wellness newsletter with a professional audience | Feature = positioned alongside trusted content |

### Who does NOT qualify (either mode)

Disqualify if any of these are true:
- No decision-maker clearly identified (who actually approves and signs off)
- Presence so weak that the result would not be credible as a case
- Partner cannot or will not authorize use of the case/logo
- Partner's audience has no overlap with PsiAtiva's ICP
- No history of follow-through (late responses, vague commitments, pattern of disappearing)
- They want everything under "free" framing — ask for full service, not narrow scope
- First reaction is "what's the catch?" without openness to honest explanation

## Step 2 — Scope Definition

**Rule: free work gets a smaller scope, not a larger one.**

The instinct is to deliver everything to compensate for not charging. The opposite is correct: a smaller scope produces a documentable result faster, limits exposure if the partner is difficult, and generates a more focused case study.

### Narrow scope options for PsiAtiva context

| Deliverable | What it proves | Ideal for |
|---|---|---|
| Google Business Profile setup + first review strategy | GBP product quality + local presence results | Portfolio cases for GBP/GBP Scale |
| Instagram bio rewrite + CTA restructure | Clareza + commercial direction | Portfolio cases for presence work |
| WhatsApp first-contact script + confirmation protocol | Process design + no-show reduction approach | Portfolio cases for process products (P3K+) |
| One landing page (captação express) | Design + conversion logic + commercial clarity | Portfolio cases for Site/LP |
| Presence audit report (written diagnosis only, no implementation) | Diagnostic quality + niche knowledge — no implementation risk | Early cases or media-for-equity as the "trade" |
| 15-day mini-project: GBP + bio + one key script | Three-component small system | Cases covering multiple problem areas |

**Media-for-equity scope options:**

The deliverable offered to a media partner should be one of the above, with the understanding that the partner is not the end client — they are the distribution channel. The real ICP is their audience.

Example: offer a free presence audit to a psychology podcast host. The audit is the deliverable. The episode where they talk about the experience + PsiAtiva's work is the payment.

### Scope must be written before the conversation starts

> *"[Deliverable específico] para [tipo de projeto/negócio], focado em resolver [gargalo único], em [prazo curto: 7–15 dias]."*

Example:
> *"Ativação de presença local no Google para a clínica, com foco em aparecer nas buscas do bairro e estruturar a primeira camada de avaliações, em 7 dias."*

If you cannot write this sentence before starting the conversation, the scope is not defined enough.

## Step 3 — Commercial Assets Checklist

These are the forms of payment. They must be agreed before work starts — not negotiated after delivery.

### Portfolio Mode assets (what to demand, in priority order)

| Asset | What it gives you | How to specify it |
|---|---|---|
| **Video testimonial** (30–60 sec) | Proof of real human result | "Um vídeo de 30 a 60 segundos contando o que estava travado, o que foi feito e o resultado percebido" |
| **Written recommendation** | Credibility text for proposals and landing page | "Um texto de 3–5 linhas que eu possa usar na minha apresentação" |
| **Case study authorization** | Right to publish the full case with name and logo | "Autorização formal para usar esse projeto como case no meu portfólio, com nome e logo de vocês" |
| **Network introductions** (2–3 specific) | Warm referrals — highest quality lead source | "Apresentação minha para 2 ou 3 pessoas da sua rede que você acha que poderiam se beneficiar do que foi feito aqui" |
| **Instagram post/story tagging PsiAtiva** | Social proof + audience exposure | "Um post ou story marcando o nosso perfil quando o projeto estiver entregue" |
| **Logo use in presentations** | Visual credibility in pitch decks | "Autorização para usar o logo de vocês na minha apresentação comercial" |

**Minimum acceptable package (portfolio mode):** video testimonial OR written recommendation + case study authorization. Without these two, the project produces no commercial asset.

### Media-for-Equity assets (what to demand, in priority order)

| Asset | What it gives you | How to specify it |
|---|---|---|
| **Instagram post to their audience** | Direct ICP exposure | "Um post no feed do seu perfil apresentando o trabalho feito, com alcance mínimo de [X] seguidores" |
| **WhatsApp group announcement** | Trusted word-of-mouth in closed environment | "Uma mensagem no grupo apresentando o que foi feito e como outras psicólogas podem falar com a gente" |
| **Podcast episode or segment** | Long-form authority + searchable content | "Um episódio ou segmento de 15–20 minutos falando sobre o processo e o resultado" |
| **Newsletter feature** | Positioned authority in trusted content | "Uma menção ou seção na sua próxima newsletter, com link direto para o nosso site" |
| **Instagram Live or collaborative content** | Dual-audience event | "Um Instagram Live de 30 minutos onde a gente fala sobre o gargalo que resolvemos e o que as psicólogas podem aprender" |
| **Audience data** | Verification that the reach is real | "Dados de alcance do post ou stories para a gente avaliar juntos o resultado" |

**Minimum acceptable package (media-for-equity mode):** one piece of content that reaches their full audience + measurable reach data. Without data, the trade is unverifiable.

## Step 4 — Pre-Agreement Checklist

Before starting any work, confirm these five terms in writing (WhatsApp message, email, or a simple document). Verbal agreement does not exist.

```
✅ Escopo: o que entra e o que explicitamente não entra
✅ Prazo: data de início e data de entrega
✅ Objetivo: qual transformação ou melhoria específica o projeto visa
✅ Contrapartida: quais ativos específicos serão entregues, em qual formato e até quando
✅ Responsabilidade do parceiro: o que você precisa deles para executar (acesso, fotos, aprovações, disponibilidade)
```

**If the partner hesitates on formalizing the contrapartida before work starts:** this is a disqualifying signal. *"Vemos depois"* and *"eu posto algo"* are not agreements — they are deferrals that disappear when the work is done.

## Step 5 — Approach Pitch Script

**Never open with:** "faço marketing e queria ajudar você de graça pra ganhar experiência."  
This positions you as a beginner looking for charity. The partner's response is pity, not partnership.

### Standard portfolio pitch

Adapted from the source article for PsiAtiva:

> *"Oi, [nome]. Dei uma olhada na presença de vocês e notei um ponto que talvez esteja travando a entrada de pacientes novos: [observação específica do diagnóstico]. Estou desenvolvendo alguns casos práticos nesse tipo de intervenção e quero selecionar poucos projetos para aplicar uma melhoria bem específica — não fazer tudo, só atacar esse ponto em [prazo curto], com escopo definido.*
>
> *Em troca, eu precisaria de autorização para usar o projeto como case, um depoimento sincero se o trabalho fizer sentido e, se houver abertura, uma apresentação para 2 ou 3 contatos da sua rede.*
>
> *Se fizer sentido, explico em 10 minutos o que eu faria exatamente."*

**Anatomy of the pitch:**
1. Specific observation (not "analisei seu perfil")
2. Named problem from the diagnostic
3. Transparent about the moment ("desenvolvendo casos práticos")
4. Narrow scope stated explicitly
5. Contrapartida listed precisely — no vague "divulgação"
6. Low-commitment CTA (10 minutes, not a full meeting)

### Media-for-equity pitch

> *"Oi, [nome]. Acompanho o seu conteúdo e vi que você fala bastante com psicólogas que estão tentando organizar a captação sem abrir mão da identidade clínica — exatamente o público que a gente ajuda na PsiAtiva.*
>
> *Tenho uma proposta: a gente faz [deliverable específico] pro seu projeto sem custo. Em troca, se o resultado fizer sentido, uma apresentação pro seu público — um post, um episódio ou um conteúdo colaborativo contando o que foi resolvido.*
>
> *A ideia não é anúncio. É mostrar na prática como uma intervenção desse tipo funciona, com um caso real. Se fizer sentido, explico como funcionaria em 15 minutos."*

**Anatomy of the pitch:**
1. Evidence you know their audience (not cold)
2. Alignment of audiences (their ICP = PsiAtiva's ICP)
3. Clear deliverable, no vague "ajuda"
4. Named payment: specific content format, not "divulgação"
5. Positioned as case/content, not advertisement
6. Low-commitment CTA

## Step 6 — Case Documentation Template

After delivery, document the project in this structure. This is the commercial asset — not a portfolio image, a proof of reasoning.

```markdown
## Case: [Partner Name / Type of Business]

### Cenário inicial
[Where the block was — specific, before any work]

### O que foi identificado
[Your diagnosis — what you saw that others might have missed]

### O que foi feito
[Deliverable, real, no smoke — what was actually implemented]

### O que mudou
[Before/after — visual, operational, or commercial change]

### O que o parceiro disse
[Verbatim testimonial — video link or written quote]

### O que esse case prova
[Positioning statement — what capability or niche knowledge this demonstrates]
```

**This structure answers the five questions a prospect asks when looking at your portfolio:**
1. Did they think about problems like mine? → Cenário inicial
2. Can they organize a solution? → O que foi identificado + O que foi feito
3. Do they execute at a minimum quality level? → O que mudou
4. Do they understand commercial logic, not just aesthetics? → O que esse case prova
5. Do I run less risk hiring them? → O que o parceiro disse

## PsiAtiva — High-Value Partner Profiles

Partners whose audience, network, or credibility directly accelerates PsiAtiva's positioning:

### Portfolio Mode (best cases)
- Established psychology clinic with 20+ Google reviews and a clear presence gap → case for GBP Scale or P3K
- Solo practitioner with 4+ years, active Instagram, visible WhatsApp process problem → case for IA Solo or P3K
- Psychology training institute or supervision group → case + access to a network of trainees

### Media-for-Equity Mode (best distribution)
- Instagram accounts focused on "psicologia clínica" or "gestão do consultório" with 5k+ engaged followers
- WhatsApp group coordinators for regional CRP chapters or specialty associations (TCC, psicanálise, etc.)
- YouTube channels or podcasts serving the "psicóloga empreendedora" content niche
- Psychology conference or event organizers (media value = speaker slot or booth placement)

## Watch out for

- **Doing free work before formalizing contrapartida** — once the work is delivered, all negotiating leverage disappears. The testimonial that was "definitely happening" suddenly takes three months or never arrives.
- **Expanding scope because it's free** — a 15-day project should not absorb 30 days of effort because the partner kept "adding one more thing." Scope creep on unpaid work is a double loss.
- **Accepting "depois eu posto algo" as the media-for-equity payment** — the post is the product. A vague promise of future content is not a media deal — it is a favor with no product.
- **Choosing a partner with no audience overlap** — a well-known clinic in a different city, a psychology professor who posts theory, a partner whose followers are students, not professionals. Reach that doesn't land on the ICP is reach with zero commercial value.
- **Not documenting the case immediately after delivery** — the partner's memory of what happened fades fast. The testimonial is best collected at delivery, not two months later.
- **Using the case pitch language of "marketing" or "atrair mais clientes"** — PsiAtiva's approach pitch should mirror PsiAtiva's brand: *"organizar o processo para quem já está buscando"*, not *"aumentar sua visibilidade digital"*.
- **Calling it "free" in the pitch** — the word "free" triggers three responses: suspicion, devaluation, or unlimited asks. Frame it as "parceria com escopo definido" or "piloto estratégico com contrapartida clara."
