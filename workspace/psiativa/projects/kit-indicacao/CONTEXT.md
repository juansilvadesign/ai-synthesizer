# Project — Kit de Indicação

## Why this exists

Cold outreach is a poor fit for PsiAtiva: this ICP's ethics filter pattern-matches unsolicited DMs as "agência de marketing" — exactly the category PsiAtiva is *not*. **Indicação (warm referral)** is the highest-trust channel available. This kit gives Juan and any future PsiAtiva-adjacent person (família, colegas, ex-clientes) a ready-to-paste asset they can share without breaking voice or positioning.

## What this kit produces

A small, self-contained set of shareable assets in PT-BR:

- **Per-ICP messages** — short WhatsApp/email copy, one variant for clínica owner and one for consultório solo. The referrer picks the variant that matches the lead.
- **Referrer template** — a short "como me apresentar" the family/colleague pastes *alongside* their own personal vouch, not as a replacement for it.
- **One-pager** — a self-contained PT-BR summary that stands on its own if the recipient never clicks the link, and serves as the source of truth for the live `/indicacao` landing page.

Landing target for every CTA: **https://psiativa.com.br/indicacao**

## Hard constraints

- **Pick one ICP per send.** Clínica e consultório solo never appear in the same message — the promise (R$10k em 6 meses vs. processo ativo em 45 dias) and emotional axis (logic+security vs. alívio+presença) are different. Mixing dilutes both. The one-pager addresses both ICPs but only because it uses a fork pattern ("se você é... / se você é..."), never blended copy.
- **Guarantee before price.** Every variant names the guarantee; price is never mentioned in the kit copy itself — that conversation belongs to the conversation that follows.
- **Hemorrhage framing only.** No improvement language ("melhore sua captação"); always concrete loss ("você está perdendo pacientes entre o primeiro contato e a sessão confirmada").
- **GAP — Gerador de Agenda Previsível** spelled in full on first mention. Briefly named, never explained.
- **Forbidden vocabulary stays out:** marketing · leads · vendas · escalar · funil · contrato · comercial · campanha · agência · cliente (do psicólogo).

## How the assets fit together

```
1. Referrer (família/colega) wants to share PsiAtiva
2. Referrer asks Juan: "qual versão mando?"
   → Juan asks: "ela tem clínica com equipe ou atende sozinha?"
3. Juan sends the matching variant from messages-clinica.md OR messages-solo.md
4. Referrer pastes the message + personal vouch (referrer-template.md as guide)
5. Recipient receives the message → clicks /indicacao
6. /indicacao serves the one-pager content live → CTA to WhatsApp diagnóstico
```
