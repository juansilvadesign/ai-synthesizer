**Campaign Structure Note: Why not divide into dozens of micro-campaigns?**
*   **Algorithm Learning:** Centralized data helps Google find the specific demographic (e.g., Leblon/Ipanema residents) faster.
*   **Budget Control:** Consolidating budget allows the system to bid aggressively on top-converting times and terms.
*   **ICP Focus:** Broadening campaigns too thinly invites L1/L2 bargain hunters. Keep it concentrated on the L3/L4 avatar.

## 7. ✅ Pre-Launch Validation Checklist

- [ ] **Campaign Goal:** Verify that the goal is set appropriately (e.g., start with "Website traffic" for initial data, but transition to "Leads" to optimize for form fills).
- [ ] **Tracking:** Is the Google Tag / Conversion Tracking actively firing on the Lead Form "Thank You" page?
- [ ] **Character Limits:** Do all headlines stay under 30 chars and descriptions under 90 chars?
- [ ] **Extensions / Assets:** Are Sitelinks, Callouts, and Structured Snippets added to maximize ad real estate?
- [ ] **Negative Keywords:** Have you added terms like "grátis", "barato", "curso", "como fazer" to the negative keyword list?
